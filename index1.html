About LoadPlus

Loadplus Private Limited (founded August 4, 2023) is a tech-enabled logistics partner based in East Delhi with a PAN-India network. We simplify your supply chain end-to-end—covering Warehousing & Fulfilment, Full Truck Load (FTL), and Global Trade / Import–Export.

Warehousing & Fulfilment: Bonded/non-bonded storage, inventory control, pick-pack, kitting and VAS with WMS visibility.
Transportation (FTL): Reliable primary movement with route planning, GPS tracking and SLA governance.
Import–Export & Customs: CHA support and documentation, customs clearance (import/export), ocean & air freight, Incoterms guidance, duties/compliance, and door-to-door multi-modal delivery.
  • Coverage: PAN-India with facilities near major ports, ICDs & metros
