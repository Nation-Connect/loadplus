What is Fly Ash

Fly Ash (pulverized fuel ash) is a byproduct of a coal-based thermal power station generated by the combustion of pulverized coal. It is a fine, grey amorphous powder, rich in silica & alumina and spherical in shape. Its properties vary based on coal type, combustion process, and plant efficiency, all of which affect its utilization quality.

Leveraging Technology to Elevate Quality

Loadplus provides Fly Ash that is highly reactive and has water-reducing characteristics. It is among the first classified and processed materials made available in India. By classifying and processing, Loadplus ensures an optimum proportion of fine particles, delivering consistent quality.

Our Fly Ash meets stringent international standards: IS EN 450 S, ASTM C 618 Class F, and IS 3812 Part 1 Grade I.

Cenosphere — Details

Lightweight, inert, hollow spheres (mostly silica & alumina) used to reduce density and thermal conductivity in cement, polymers, coatings, and composites.

  • Typical bulk density ~0.4–0.8 g/cm³
  • Improves flow, reduces shrinkage, lowers resin use
  • Enhances thermal and acoustic insulation
